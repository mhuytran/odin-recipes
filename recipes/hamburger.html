Hamburger

Description

The origins of the hamburger are debated and reflect the multicultural roots of the United States. Some say the hamburger originated in Hamburg, German, while others argue German-American immigrants were the first to serve fried ground beef in a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Bread Buns
  • Salad Slices
  • Tomato and Onions

Steps

  1. Toast the buns
  2. Grill the patty of the buns
  3. Cut the Salad into Slices
  4. Tomato and Onions
