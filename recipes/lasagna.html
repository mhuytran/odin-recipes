Lasagna

Description

The origins of lasagna are disputed, with claims from Italy, Greece, England. The closest version to modern lasagna is though to have originated in Naples, Italy during the Middle Ages, with the first recorded recipe dating back to the 14th century. The traditional Neapolitan version, lasagne di carnevale, is layered with local sausage, meatballs, hard-boiled eggs, ricotta or mozzarella cheese, and Neapolitan ragu.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
